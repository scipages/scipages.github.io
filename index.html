Easy to Use

Generate a SEO friendly static website with the press of a button. SciPages lets you focus on your content and it takes care of everything else.

Multiple Website Types

SciPages is focused on academic websites (for personal profiles or research labs, courses, conferences/workshops, etc.). Nevertheless, it can also be used for any other kind of static website.

Open Source / Cross Platform

SciPages is an open source project developed in Electron, TypeScript, Vue.js+Quasar and runs on Windows, Linux and macOS.
